Spatially Varying Color Editing

ICE-NeRF offers a notable advantage in its ability to recolor each component of a scene with distinct colors. Our method is capable of changing the colors of multiple objects with similar colors or semantics into different colors (Horns scene), as well as changing a single object into multiple colors (Flower scene).

SMR Multi-View Mask Examples

The proposed SMR utilizes the user's single mask input by rendering it into multiple views. Typically, in training, up to 5 additional views are rendered and used. Here, to demonstrate the well-generated multi-view masks, we show the rendered masks from 120 consecutive views.
